Claire Beardsley was cleaning out the attic in an old Southern California Craftsman House and discovered a box full of papers, letters, and documents gathered by a woman who was active in The Women's Suffrage Movement. In it was a signed letter by the woman, who, at the time was a Famous leader in the movement, Alice Paul. Claire Beardsley was, herself active in the Women's Movement in the 60s and thus, was excited to find this box of over a thousand documents.
